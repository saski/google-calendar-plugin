# Chrome Web Store Listing

## Name
Google Calendar Full-Year View

## Short Description (132 characters max)
Adds a full-year view to Google Calendar with sequential day layout for better long-term planning

## Detailed Description (16,000 characters max)

### Overview
Google Calendar Full-Year View enhances your calendar experience by adding a comprehensive full-year visualization to Google Calendar. Unlike the standard monthly or weekly views, this extension displays all 365 days in a sequential, easy-to-scan format that makes long-term planning effortless.

### Key Features

**Sequential Day Layout**
- View all 365 days of the year in a single, scrollable interface
- Days are displayed in chronological order, making it easy to see your entire year at a glance
- Perfect for identifying patterns, gaps, and opportunities in your schedule

**Seamless Integration**
- Works directly within Google Calendar - no need to switch between applications
- Automatically syncs with your existing Google Calendar events
- Respects your calendar's privacy settings and permissions

**Event Visualization**
- All your events are displayed in the full-year view
- Hover over events to see quick details
- Click events to view full information
- Color-coded events match your Google Calendar color scheme

**Easy Navigation**
- Quick access button in the Google Calendar interface
- Toggle between standard views and full-year view instantly
- Smooth scrolling through the entire year

### Use Cases

**Long-Term Planning**
- Plan projects that span multiple months
- Identify busy periods and plan accordingly
- Schedule important events with full context of the year

**Event Coordination**
- See all events at once to avoid conflicts
- Plan around existing commitments
- Identify optimal dates for new events

**Visual Overview**
- Get a complete picture of your year
- Spot patterns in your schedule
- Balance work and personal commitments

### How It Works

1. **Install the Extension**
   - Add the extension to Chrome from the Chrome Web Store
   - Grant necessary permissions for calendar access

2. **Authenticate with Google**
   - Sign in with your Google account
   - Grant read-only access to your calendar

3. **Access Full-Year View**
   - Navigate to Google Calendar
   - Click the "Full Year View" button
   - Browse your entire year in one view

### Privacy & Security

- **Read-Only Access**: The extension only reads your calendar data - it never modifies or deletes events
- **Local Processing**: All data processing happens locally in your browser
- **No Data Transmission**: Your calendar data is never sent to external servers
- **OAuth Security**: Uses Google's secure OAuth 2.0 authentication
- **Open Source**: Full source code available for review

### Technical Details

- Built with Manifest V3 for modern Chrome compatibility
- Lightweight and performant
- No external dependencies
- Regular updates and improvements
